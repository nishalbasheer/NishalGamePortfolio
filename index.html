I'm Nishal, and ever since my childhood, my aim has been to become a game developer. Whenever I play games, the first thing that catches my eye is the design, architecture, artwork, and environments within the game. From my 10th grade, I began creating my first game, a 2D side-scroller, which ignited my passion for game development. Having recently completed my BCA, I have developed numerous games using Unreal Engine and Unity, leveraging tutorials and resources from Google and YouTube. With proper guidance, I am confident in my ability to become a skilled game developer. I am actively seeking opportunities to further my career.
