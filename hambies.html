Hambies Acreage

Game Overview

Hambies Acreage is an open-world survival game and my first 3D project developed using Unity. Players are thrust into a vast, unforgiving landscape where they must gather resources, craft tools, and defend themselves against threats using a variety of weapons. The game combines exploration, combat, and survival as players navigate this dangerous world

Gameplay Mechanics

Players can find and use a range of weapons, including guns, to defend against enemies. The car system allows for faster travel across the game’s diverse terrain, while the health system requires players to monitor and manage their well-being, balancing hunger, injury, and fatigue

Visual Design

The game’s stylized visuals capture the ruggedness of the wilderness, with detailed environments that range from dense forests to open plains

Development Process

Hambies Acreage was developed in Unity as my first 3D game, with a focus on integrating survival mechanics, combat systems, and vehicle physics.
